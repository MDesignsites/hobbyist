About Us

Advanced Hobbyist Genetic Breeding Station was organized on March 9, 2002, and later incorporated on July 1, 2005.

The primary objective of Advanced Hobbyist Genetic Breeding Station was to establish breeding farms as a hobby while generating income. Jaime "Mr. Jimi" Lim, driven by his passion for genetic breeding, founded the company. His dedication is evident in every aspect, from the meticulous selection of breeding stock to advanced techniques in the breeding process. Under Mr. Jimi's leadership, the company has grown from a hobby into a reputable business, providing a platform for enthusiasts and contributing to advancements in genetic breeding.

This blend of hobby and enterprise has created a unique niche, attracting hobbyists and investors alike. Read more about the fascinating journey of Advanced Hobbyist Genetic Breeding Station and how Mr. Jimi Lim's passion continues to drive its success.
